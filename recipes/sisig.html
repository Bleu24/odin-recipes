Sisig Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Chopped Pork
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 Chopped Onion
  • 1 chopped chili
  • 15g Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Pour oil to pan
  2. Saute onion and chili peppers
  3. Cook chopped pork
  4. Add 2 eggs and mix them all
  5. Add seasoning to your liking
  6. Add 15 grams of Mayonnaise
  7. Serve and enjoy!
