Scrambled Egg Recipe

Description

Simple but delicious scrambled egg recipe!

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • 3 eggs
  • Magic Sarap

Steps

  1. Crack three eggs
  2. Scramble them thoroughly
  3. Add a sprinkle of Magic Sarap
  4. Pour oil to pan in low heat
  5. Once pan is hot, pour scrambled egg
  6. Scramble them thoroughly, avoid overcooking it
  7. Serve and enjoy!
